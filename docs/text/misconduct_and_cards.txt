Misconduct and Cards (Soccer)

1. Yellow Card: Given for dissent, delaying restarts, reckless fouls, or unsporting behavior.
2. Red Card: Given for serious foul play, violent conduct, denying a goal with a handball, or receiving a second yellow card.
3. Player Removal: A sent-off player must leave the field and cannot return.
4. Team Officials: Coaches or staff can also receive cards for irresponsible behavior.
5. Celebrations: Excessive or unsafe celebrations may result in a caution.
6. Restart After Card: Play restarts based on the original reason for the whistle unless otherwise required.
