Referees and Officials (Soccer)

1. Referee Role: The referee enforces the Laws of the Game and makes final decisions. The referee has the power to show yellow or red cards.
2. Assistant Referees: Help indicate offside, out-of-play decisions, and fouls.
3. Fourth Official: Manages substitutions, extra time displays, and technical area behavior.
4. VAR: Video Assistant Referees may assist with major decisions like goals and penalties. GLT systems may be used to verify whether a goal has been scored to support the refereeâ€™s decision.
5. Authority: Referees oversee player equipment, injuries, and match safety.
6. Signals: Referees and assistants use approved hand signals to communicate decisions.
