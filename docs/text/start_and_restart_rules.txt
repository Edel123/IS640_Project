Start and Restart Rules (Soccer)

1. Kick-Off: Used to start the match, start the second half, and restart after goals.
2. Dropped Ball: Used when the referee stops play and no other restart applies.
3. Throw-In: Awarded when the ball crosses the touchline. Taken by the team that did not touch it last.
4. Goal Kick: Awarded when the attacking team last touches the ball before it crosses the goal line.
5. Corner Kick: Awarded when the defending team last touches the ball before it crosses the goal line.
6. Free Kicks: Awarded after fouls. They can be direct or indirect depending on the offense.
7. Penalty Kick: Awarded for certain fouls inside the penalty area.
